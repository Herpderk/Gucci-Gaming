
Please set your Steam profile to public. To do so, follow these steps:

1. Open your Steam Client, click [View my profile], then the [Edit Profile] link under your displayed badge.
2. Click the "My Privacy Settings" tab.
3. Select the following Settings: My Profile: Public & Game Details: Public.
4. Click the "Save" button.

Once you have performed these steps, please relaunch the program and try again.
